## Punctuation/Style Quick Reference

- No em dashes (—) in copy. Use a comma, period, or colon.
- No double hyphens ( -- ) as dash substitutes.
- No ellipsis (… or ...) or &hellip; except in UI loading states or utility like read more links.
- Oxford comma always: put a comma before "and" or "or" conjunction.
- No formatted quotes (“ or ”) or &ldquo; &rdquo; style. Use " or &quot; always.
- No exclamation points unless genuinely earned or required for tone/stance (like error or warning clause).
- Sentence case on all headings and labels - no Title Case For Everything.
- Limited use of bold and italic spans. Save these for when absolutely needed, so they pop, instead of getting lost in a sea of other bold/italic items.
